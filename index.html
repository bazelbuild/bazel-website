Bazel is an open-source build and test tool which is correct, fast and scalable by design.

Bazel is Google's build tool, now available to everyone. Bazel makes builds reproducible and only rebuilds what is necessary.
It has built-in support for building both client and server software in a variety of programming languages, and is easily extensible.

Why Bazel?

Reduce build times

Speed up your builds and tests: With advanced local and distributed caching, optimized dependency analysis and parallel execution, you get fast and incremental builds.

One tool, multiple languages

Build and test Java, C++, Android, iOS, Go and a wide variety of other language platforms. Bazel runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Scalable

Bazel helps you scale your organization, codebase and Continuous Integration system. It handles codebases of any size, in multiple repositories or a huge monorepo.

Extensible to your needs

Easily add support for new languages and platforms with Bazel's familiar extension language. Share and re-use language rules written by the growing Bazel community.

Trusted by industry leaders
